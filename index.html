THE ARTISTS BEHIND THE LENS

At Vinayak Studio, we don't just capture photographs — we celebrate love in its most beautiful, raw, and unforgettable form. Since 2004, we have been the trusted storytellers of Neem Ka Thana's most cherished celebrations. From intimate family gatherings to grand royal weddings, 600+ families have placed their faith in our lens.

Founded with a dream to turn life's most precious moments into timeless works of art, our journey is built on trust earned over two decades. Combining traditional heart with cinematic craft, every frame we create is a chapter in your family's legacy — as vivid and magical as the day itself felt.
